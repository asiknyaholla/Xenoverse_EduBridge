Empowering Rural Karnataka Students

EduBridge is a smart learning access platform for 10th grade students in rural Karnataka. Learn subjects like English, Kannada, Math, Science, Social Science, Sanskrit and Hindi with simple, exam-focused content.

About EduBridge

EduBridge bridges the digital divide by giving rural students easy access to quality learning resources. Students get structured folders by subject, clear video lessons, and simple notes that match their 10th standard syllabus.

Our goal is to make learning simple, clear and reachable from any village in Karnataka.
